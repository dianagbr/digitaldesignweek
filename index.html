DIGITAL DESIGN WEEK 2021

Creative's favourite event of the year

Digital Design Week is a project created for the Web Design class but supposedly, it tries to be a one-week festival in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, that promotes the development of local design and creative industries. The agenda includes conferences, workshops and plenty of community activities.

The festival revolves around various themes such as UX/UI Design, Graphic Design, Interaction and Motion Design, Art Direction for Web, Digital Experiences and Visual Languages and more.

In 2021 attendees will be able to participate both online and in person. Ticket prices vary based on this criteria.
